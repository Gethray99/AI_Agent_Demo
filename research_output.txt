Large language model (LLM) is a computational model designed to understand and generate human-like text. It is trained on massive amounts of text data, allowing it to learn patterns, grammar, and factual information. LLMs can be used for a variety of natural language processing tasks, including text generation, translation, summarization, and question answering.
